## Genetic Load Analysis
Two-script pipeline for analyzing deleterious mutation burden in 100-kb windows:
1.  **Thresholding Script:** Identifies genomic regions where the *minimum* deleterious burden across *Candolleanum* (CND) accessions is lower than in elite lines (A626/E463).
2.  **Optimization Script:** Implements a greedy selection algorithm to identify the most influential CND samples ($k=2$–$51$) by maximizing the reduction of the aggregate burden.
Results highlight core accessions responsible for the majority of the genetic load.
